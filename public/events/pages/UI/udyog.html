Udyog Details

For the budding chemical engineers, Udyog gives you the platform to showcase your core chemical engineering skills. The basic concepts of chemical engineering, along with an analytical mind, is what you need to sail through. This year Udyog will be organized entirely online.

You will be given a problem statement about the current ongoing crisis the world is facing. You have to solve for the situations that are mentioned in the problem statement under challenges to participants.
We expect you to participate in large numbers as this is something you will enjoy solving.

Timeline
  • Registration will start on 26th March 2020.
  • Problem Statement will be released on 27th March 2020.
  • You will be participating in a team of at most 3 members. More than three are strictly not allowed.
  • You will have first to submit the abstract, which will contain a brief description of your approach towards the solution, and the qualifiers of the abstract round will have to submit their solutions by making a PPT.
  • You must adhere to all the rules and constraints mentioned in the problem statement.
  • Detailed timelines and rules will be mentioned in the problem statement. Till then, Stay Tuned!!!
